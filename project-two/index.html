From Our Hearth To Yours.......

Welcome to Sweet Delights Bakery. We are a small family-owned business that has been in operation for over 50 years. We specialize in hand-crafted baked goods made from scratch, such as cakes, breads, sweet rolls, cookies, and many other sweet delights to tickle your tastebuds. We are located in the heart of downtown Crystal Lake. We are only a few minutes walk from the train station. During our many years of service we continue to use only the finest ingredients. Our recipes have been passed down from generation to generation in our family and are tried and true. Below is just a small list of some of our best selling and most famous delights enjoyed by our returning patrons.

  • Cinful Cinnamon Rolls
  • Perfectly Pumpkin Pie*
  • Magnificent Molasses Cookies*
  • Not-So-Sour Sourdough Bread
  • Red Velvet Cake*
  • Chock-ful Chocolate Chip Cookies*

Our goal is to provide you with the best-tasting baked goods possible at an affordable price. We purchase many of our ingredients from local farms in an effort to reduce our carbon footprint and continually participate in local commerce. We also offer a wide variety of Gluten-Free delights. Unable to stop in? We have multiple delivery methods available for Crystal Lake and our surrounding McHenry County communities. By shopping with us, you allow us to give back to the community through weekly donations to the local food pantry. Our community is our bread and butter and we want to return the flavor.

We hope you visit us soon so we may impress you with more than just photos! A wonderful world of taste and smell awaits!
