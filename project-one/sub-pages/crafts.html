Crafts

I enjoy crafts such as knitting, crocheting and cross-stitch. Currently I am knitting a Doctor Who scarf for my husband. I found the pattern to one of the scarves worn by Tom Baker during his portrayal of the Doctor, on the web. The pattern is linked below for anyone "WHO" is interested in acquiring the pattern. Once I have completed the scarf for my husband the next project will be cross-stitching a humorous pattern (photo of the pattern below) from the British television show "The IT Crowd" for my husband who works as desktop support. After that I plan on continuing with a mixed granny square quilt I started a few years ago. I have a lot of projects to keep me busy for a while.
