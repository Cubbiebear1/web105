Amature Photography

This image was taken this summer during the Canada wildfires of 2023. The haze from the fires created an additional filter to create this effect. I took the picture from the college campus parking lot while on my break with my phone. I am always looking for a potential opportunity to take pictures.


This photograph was taken from a dock on the side of a highway in Switzerland in 1995. At the age of eighteen, I was given the opportunity to stay in Germany for six months earning my room and board by working in the kitchen of a retreat center in Vallendar Germany. During my time in Germany,I was offered an opportunity to join a weekend bus trip down to Switzerland. I asked the bus driver to stop as we were driving by this lake so I could take a picture. To my surprise the bus driver obliged and double parked on the highway so I could get this shot. This picture was taken at the end of a dock that lead into this lake. My only regret was that I did not have a better quality camera for this photo.
